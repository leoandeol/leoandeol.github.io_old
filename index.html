Biography

I am a second-year Master’s student at École Normale Supérieure Paris-Saclay in Applied Mathematics, studying Machine Learning. I received my Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science from Université Paris-Dauphine in 2018 and followed the first year of my Master’s degree at Sorbonne Université. I spent the summer 2019 as a Research Intern in RIKEN, at the Tokyo Institute of Technology. I am particularly interested in general aspects of ML, I have worked on Transfer Learning, Graph Neural Networks, Adversarial Training, Active learning and more. I am currently looking for a PhD program for Fall 2020.
